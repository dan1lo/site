Ph.D Danilo Monteiro

Hi everyone! My name is Danilo. I am Ph.D. in Computer Science at Center of Informatics of Federal University of Pernambuco (2020). Currently, I am Software Engineering Researcher and Data Scientist at Zup Innovation . My main research interests are Human Aspects in Software Engineering, Education and Training in Software Engineering. Previous, I was Professor at some universities like the University of Pernambuco, Federal Rural University of Pernambuco (EAD), UNINABUCO, Faculty of Philosophy Sciences and Letters of Caruaru and UNINASSAU.
